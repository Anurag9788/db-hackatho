2024 KanTime Experience

Join us August 26-28, 2024 in Dallas, Texas, for our annual KanTime Experience!

KanTime’s Superheros for Success Annual Experience is a nexus for the best and brightest in post acute care, bringing together a diverse group of individuals who are redefining innovation within home care delivery. In this time of uncertain market shifts and an unprecedented demand for quality home-based care, the shared knowledge of our community is more crucial than ever. Our Superheros for Success Annual KanTime Experience will update users on the latest KanTime solutions, provide tips for optimizing KanTime software to meet changing needs, offer clinical continuing education units (CEUs), and share expert insights into evolving regulations and processes within the post acute care community.
